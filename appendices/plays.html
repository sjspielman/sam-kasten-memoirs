Appendix B: Glossary of plays

This appendix contains a table of Yiddish Theater plays that Sam mentions, either as a play he performed in, attended, or refers to someone else performing in. Plays are listed in order of first appearance.

In the Playwright/composer(s) column, I provide names of those who were documented to have been involved writing the play and/or music. An absence of a composer or lyricist does not imply that the main playwright necessary also wrote the songs; in many cases, this information is just not definitively known (or I havenâ€™t found it yet!). It was also common for songs to be added to plays by additional songwriters who were not officially credited.
